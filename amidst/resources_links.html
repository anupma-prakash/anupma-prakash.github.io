Related education projects by Anupma Prakash
 
EDGE: NSF GeoED funded project on imparting geology and geospatial education to Alaska teachers and students.
Treasure Hunt in Alaska: Introduces the concepts of radar remote sensing and Earth science to students from grades 3-5 in story form. NASA approved.
Alaska: A Bird's Eye View: Story introducing facts about Alaska and the concepts of remote sensing to students from grades 4-5. NASA approved.
Shrinking Forest Growing Problem: Case study on monitoring deforestation using Synthetic Aperture Radar data for high school students. NSF funded.
Polar Remote Sensing: Information about the International Polar Year and a case study of an Alaskan glacier for undergraduate audience.

Fire Ecology: Resource Management Education Unit elaborating on the natural role of fires in the environment

Gold: 23-page kids oriented USGS publication. Includes a brief history of the "gold fever" that has endured for centuries of civilizations.
Gold Rush : Basic Information with a lot of external links to explore further.
