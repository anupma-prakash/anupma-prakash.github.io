K12 education

I have a strong interest in educational outreach, especially to a K-12 audience. My recent effort in this direction resulted in a web-based story for grade 5-8 children to teach them about remote sensing and some facts about Alaska. This project was supported by the Alaska Space Grant Program. The website can be viewed at http://www.uaf.edu/asgp/k12.
