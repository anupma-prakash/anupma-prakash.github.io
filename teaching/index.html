Teaching

For two decades (1998 to 2018) I was deeply involved in teaching undergraduate, graduate and professional development courses. These ranged from formal courses in the Netherlands and the United States, to tailored workshops different parts of the world.

Courses that I routinely taught include:

  • GEOS 378: Introduction to Geoinformatics
  • GEOS 422: Geoscience Applications of Remote Sensing
  • GEOS 458: Geoscience applications of GPS and GIS
  • GEOS 595: GIS/GPS for Alaska Teachers
  • GEOS 654: Visible and Infrared Remote Sensing
