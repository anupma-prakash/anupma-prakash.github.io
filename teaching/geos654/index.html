GEOS 654: Visible and Infrared Remote Sensing
This is an advanced remote sensing course that builds on a basic remote sensing course such as GEOS 422. The focus of this course is on the physical principles of remote sensing with an emphasis on remote sensing in the visible and infrared regions of the electromagnetic spectrum.
