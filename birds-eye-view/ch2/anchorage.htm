This is a 'false color' image of Anchorage. The colors are called "false" because surface features appear very different than we usually observe in reality. On the image, vegetation (trees, grass, shrubs, etc.) appears in different shades of red. Man-made objects (buildings and roads) appear in cyan-blue shades. The rectangular buildings arranged in systematic rows along roads give a typical checkered pattern to the image, which is characteristic of any big city.
