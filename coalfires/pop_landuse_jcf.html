This is the landuse map of a part of the Jharia Coalfield. The Damodar river is on the Southwest corner of this map. The numbers 1 to 6 represent dense and sparse vegetation, fire, opencast mining, overburden dump, subsidence/barren and settlement areas, respectively. For details on criteria for identifying landuse classes on digitally processed images refer to the article "Land-use mapping and change detection in a coal mining area - a case study of the Jharia Coalfield, India".
