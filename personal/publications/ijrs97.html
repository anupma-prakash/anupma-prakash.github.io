A Landsat TM based comparative study of surface and subsurface fires in the Jharia Coalfield, India

A. Prakash, R.P. Gupta and A.K. Saraf
Department of Earth Sciences, University of Roorkee, Roorkee 247 667, India


published in
International Journal of Remote Sensing, 18(11), 2463-2469.
Copyright © 1997 by Taylor & Francis Ltd.

Abstract

Landsat TM data were used to detect surface and subsurface fires in the Jharia coalfield (JCF). TM6 was useful for mapping subsurface fires and TM5 and TM7 were useful for mapping surface fires. The distribution pattern of fires was studied and their temperature and areal extents were estimated. A comparison of the distribution of surface and subsurface coal fires indicated that at some sites, fires occur only on the surface or only in the subsurface, while at other sites both surface and subsurface fires occur.
