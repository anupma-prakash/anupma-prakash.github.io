AADHAAR

Since 1997 I have been working closely with AADHAAR, a group primarily of non-resident Indians based in the Netherlands. The group meets regularly and contributes financially to support non-government organisations in India involved with social work.

The AADHAAR website provides a brief insight into the projects which the group is currently supporting.
